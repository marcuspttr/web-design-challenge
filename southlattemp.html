Analysis: Max Temperature


In the southern hemisphere we can see a city's latitude and max experienced temperature is a fairly strong, linear relationship. In general we are not surprised as we are moving towards the equator, but the strength is remarkable with a correlation coeffecient of r = 0.83. Furthermore an interesting statistical test would be to test the means to see if there is a significant different between the northern and southern hemispheres. Whether this difference is due to the Earth's tilt, location of the sampled cities, or other factors could then be explored.
